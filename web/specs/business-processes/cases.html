# Screen design

# Editing client data

while much of the client data displayed in the case, technically it lives within the client's record. We should offer a very simple way to "in-line" edit those fields in the case.

This is because as the case is being taken in by staff, they usually confirm client data such as their phone number. If anything changed, they should be able to enter it there and then, and not have to open a separate screen.
