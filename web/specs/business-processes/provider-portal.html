  • id, client name, date
  • download / print PDF

# Authorization requests (OTRs)

Providers can submit an OTR with an online form. This is the digitized version of this form:

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xxOTAF5VRxPoSq_boWH4Si7-gGy0pyAU/view?usp=sharing (opens new window)

The form is largely unchanged, the main difference being providers do not manually enter client information but check from a dropdown of those clients they have been previously authorized to treat within the last 6(?) months.IDs, birthday etc. is thus automatically filled out. No need to enter their provider information too, of course, as they are logged in as the portal.

# Updating contact info, addresses etc.

A simple form to update their info at any time.

Notifications will be sent to all intake staff whenever a change is made.
